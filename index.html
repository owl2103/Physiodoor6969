Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of conditions including back pain, neck pain, shoulder pain, knee pain, post-surgery rehabilitation, sports injuries, neurological conditions, geriatric care, and respiratory issues. Our certified physiotherapist provides personalized treatment plans for each condition.

How do home visits work?

Our physiotherapist comes to your home with all necessary equipment. We conduct a thorough assessment, create a personalized treatment plan, and provide hands-on therapy in the comfort of your own space. Sessions typically last 45-90 minutes depending on your needs.

What are your charges?

Our charges vary based on the type of treatment and session duration. Single sessions range from ₹800-1200. We also offer cost-effective packages: Weekly Package (6 sessions) for ₹4,499 and Monthly Package (26 sessions) for ₹19,999. Insurance coverage is available for eligible treatments.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. We can help you understand your coverage and submit claims for eligible treatments. Please contact us with your insurance details for verification.

How soon can I get an appointment?

Regular appointments can usually be scheduled within 24-48 hours. For urgent cases, we offer same-day appointments when available. Emergency consultations can be arranged within a few hours of your call.

What should I prepare for the session?

Please wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes that allow easy movement. Have a clean, quiet space ready for the session. Keep any medical reports, X-rays, or previous treatment records handy. We'll bring all necessary equipment.
